Square 1 Café: Community, Business, Connection.

A relaxed, till‑free community café where everything is free and everyone is welcome— offering hot drinks, light lunch, a warm space to pause, and real conversations that reduce loneliness and build social connection.

Not‑for‑profit Community‑led Business & residents together Students & local people

What happens at a Square 1 Café?

Step out of the workshop, lecture theatre, or home—and into a local “bumping space” where residents, workers and students meet as equals.

  • Free hot drinks & free light lunch
  • A warm, relaxed, alcohol‑free social space
  • Board games, music and low‑pressure conversation
  • Connections that build trust and community wellbeing
